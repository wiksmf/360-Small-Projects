According to Wikipedia, the factorial of a positive integer n, denoted by n!, is the product of all positive integers less than or equal to n:

n! = 1 * 2 * 3 * (n + 1) * (n + 2) * (n + 3) * ..... * n

Examples:

  • 3! = 1 * 2 * 3 = 6
  • 5! = 1 * 2 * 3 * 4 * 5 = 120
  • 7! = 1 * 2 * 3 * 4 * 5 * 6 * 7 = 5040
