According to Wikipedia, a happy number is a number which eventually reaches 1 when replaced by the sum of the square of each digit.

Examples:

  • 13 = 12 + 32 = 10 and
    • 10 = 12 + 02 = 1
  • 19 = 12 + 92 = 82 and
    • 82 = 82 + 22 = 68 and
      • 68 = 62 + 82 = 100 and
        • 100 = 12 + 02 + 02 = 1
